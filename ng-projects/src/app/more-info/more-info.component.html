WHO AM I

Byron Labuschagne

Born in Kwa-Zulu Natal, South Africa. I was first introduced to coding in 2016 where I learned HTML and CSS and straight after I learned Wordpress. My knowledge was further expanded when I join Netwise Multi-Media in 2018 and worked solely on CMS sites (mainly Joomla and on occasion Wordpress) building custom css design structures with bootstrap according to the client's needs.

I later worked at Neighbiz as a UI/UX designer and frontend developer. My design stack was mainly Flinto (for prototyping) and Sketch (for design and wireframing), but later worked with Figma and Adobe XD. I was the only designer at the company. On the frontend side I collaborated in a team of 5, my role involved building Vue.js components where needed, we maintained our code within a git repo and team sprints were managed within Jira and Toggl.
